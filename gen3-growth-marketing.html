The Story

Through our growth marketing framework, gen@3, Kerala's traditional overseas education and immigration consultancy, amplified online reach to 927.9K+ and generated 17 qualified inbound leads.

With over 20 years of offline success, gen@3 had established itself as a trusted name in overseas education and immigration. But in today's market, credibility isn't enough if you aren't visible online.

Despite their reputation, their digital presence told a different story:

  • Weak branding: No cohesive identity that carried their offline trust online.
  • Unclear messaging: Website and social media failed to connect emotionally with students or immigrants.
  • Website friction: Poor navigation and weak CTAs drove bounce rates.
  • Lack of focus: With limited resources, efforts were scattered and results negligible.

We repositioned gen@3 as a credible digital-first consultancy, turning their 20+ years of offline expertise into a scalable growth engine for leads, visibility, and conversions.

What worked well

01

Brand clarity

We repositioned gen@3 to highlight its 20+ years of expertise while aligning the identity with the aspirations of students and immigrants. This gave the brand authority online and ensured every touchpoint built trust instead of confusion.

02

Audience alignment

By mapping personas for both students and immigrants, we uncovered goals, fears, and decision triggers. This allowed us to refine messaging and focus on high-impact platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and webinars where these audiences actively seek guidance.

03

Conversion engine

We turned gen@3's website into a growth asset by revamping copy, refining CTAs, streamlining navigation, and implementing a high-intent keyword strategy. These improvements boosted discoverability and ensured that visitors converted into qualified leads.
