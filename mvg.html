Validate what works before you scale what doesn’t.

When you’re building from 0→1, you don’t need more marketing you need clarity that makes marketing work. Most early-stage founders set up full-scale campaigns before they need them, and end up burning time and financial runway.

The Minimum Viable Growth (MVG) Plan helps you build the fundamentals of scalable marketing before investing in large-scale execution. It’s the groundwork every brand needs to scale with confidence.

Over a focused 4-6 months phase, we help you identify your ideal audience, refine your positioning, and set up a repeatable system that builds consistent traction. After this foundation is in place, our clients transition seamlessly into our Growth Marketing Program through launch or post-launch.

MVG helps you identify what’s working before you spend big, ensuring every next dollar, post, or campaign builds on proof, not assumptions. If you have a promising product, some early traction, but inconsistent growth, MVG helps you move from “we’re building” to “we’re growing.”

It’s ideal for

  • Founders building from 0→1 and preparing for GTM or fundraising
  • Tech and D2C startups refining their product–market fit
  • Lean teams that want clarity before scaling marketing investments
