The story

Team Counsel is a legal consultancy built for founders, helping startups navigate everything from early compliance to growth-stage legal strategy.

Founder John Mathew brought more than a decade of legal expertise and credibility. Yet, like many service-led founders, he faced a frustrating reality: his reputation wasn’t translating into growth.

Founders only came to him in emergencies, treating legal as a last-minute Band-Aid rather than a strategic partner. Referrals drove most of his business, but there was no system for visibility, trust, or lead generation at scale.

Team Counsel faced three critical gaps:

  • Positioning gap: John’s expertise wasn’t framed as a differentiated, founder-first narrative.
  • Audience disconnect: Messaging felt reactive and complex, leaving startup founders unengaged.
  • System gap: Without a content engine, website clarity, or conversion assets, scaling beyond referrals was impossible.

We repositioned Team Counsel as a proactive ally for founders, turning legal into a growth engine for credibility, visibility, and high-ticket conversions.

What worked well

01

Brand positioning

We reframed the consultancy as a proactive ally, shifting legal from “compliance” to “confidence,” a narrative founders could instantly connect with.

02

Website revamp

We transformed the website into a credibility engine with founder-first language and conversion assets that reflected startup founders’ real concerns.

03

Founder visibility

We translated John’s 10+ years of expertise into a voice of authority on LinkedIn, simplifying complex legal topics into approachable insights.

04

Conversion assets

We rebuilt pitch decks and collateral focusing on ROI and urgency, making the value obvious within seconds to founders and VCs.

05

Targeted outreach

We launched focused campaigns aimed at founders and VCs in India and the USA, validating that the new narrative resonated across geographies.

06

Market validation

A surge in impressions and closed high-ticket deals validated that legal could be positioned as a scaling partner, not just a reactive service.
